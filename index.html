Running Trends Website

The Running Maps website was created in a group effort to analyze running trends in the Boston area, with the aim of learning whether greenery and pedestrian density impact where people run. After retrieving thousands of running routes from the MapMyRun API, we displayed our results on a density map, and made similar maps of tree density and pedestrian density. By comparing the three, the viewer can clearly see a correlation between both pedestrian density, tree density, and runner frequency.

Boston Green Spaces Map

The green spaces map was made as a personal project to aid in my own exploring, and to show distinctions between the many districts and neighborhoods of Boston. Some areas are very green and speckled with small buildings. Others are white and barren except for large warehouses and convention centers. The map makes the differences distinct, clearly showing the viewer lines between urban pedestrian-friendly areas, industrial districts, and suburban neighborhoods.

Randomly Generated Maze
